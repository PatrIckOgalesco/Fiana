About Fiana

Our Mission

Fiana is an AI-powered chatbot designed to enhance user engagement through responsive and intuitive interactions. Built as an Group project, Fiana is crafted to provide quick, accurate responses across a wide array of topics. Powered by the advanced Gemini API, Fiana offers seamless communication, adapting to user inquiries with precision and speed.

The chatbot has been developed with a focus on accessibility and usability, making it an ideal virtual assistant for answering frequently asked questions and providing support. Fiana embodies a blend of intelligence and user-friendliness, making complex information easily accessible and helping users find answers efficiently.

Features Limitation

    Gemini, like other AI models, has limitations on the types of requests it can process. Here are some of the topics that are generally prohibited or restricted:

  • Harmful and Illegal Content: Hate Speech, Violence, Self-Harm, Illegal Activities
  • Sensitive and Private Information: Personal Information, Confidential Information
  • Misinformation and Disinformation: False or Misleading Information, Conspiracy Theories
  • Harmful or Biased Content: Stereotypes, Discrimination

    • Free Gemini API Limitation

    The free tier of the Gemini API has the following limitations:

    Gemini also imposes rate limits on incoming requests to prevent abuse. For public API entry points, the recommendation is to not exceed 1 request per second. For private API entry points, the recommendation is to not exceed 5 requests per second.

  • 10 RPM (requests per minute)
  • 10,000 TPM (tokens per minute)
  • 1,000 RPD (requests per day)
